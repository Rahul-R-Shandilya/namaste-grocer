About Us


Founded in 2022, we are a B2B firm focused at providing a timely delivery of fine grade staple food as per the customer needs. At every level of the business, we work to satisfy the needs of our customers. NamasteGrocer believes the food procurement process is evolving over time and we aim to make this process hassle-free to you, so you get to focus on your core business without worrying about the secondary activities like procurement and delivery.


From a roadside shop to big supermarkets, from e-commerce to hospitality industry, food procurement has been and will always be one of the most important elements of supply chain management, which is essentially buying the correct ingredients in right quantity, at the best time and the affordable price, and this is where NamasteGrocer comes into play. We will procure the best quality food items as per your needs and deliver to you at the requested time and location for a great deal.

Vision And Mission


A healthy food procurement and service delivery establishes nutrition criteria to increase the availability of foods that promote healthy diets. Our vision is to supply the highest-quality food products at the most competitive prices to every corner of the country, so no customer has to struggle with the food procurement process in their business. We promote healthy eating for a better tomorrow.
